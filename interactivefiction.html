The Application:

Hit 'console' and then 'run' to start playing.
What our app is about:

Prompted with a task to create an interactive fiction game, we decided to do a quest-themed game, a bank heist. Our bank heist provides the user with multiple different ending possibilities such as escaping via helicopter, getting caught by the police, or living a free life in another country. Our goal was to create a fun experience for all users. Hope you enjoy!
